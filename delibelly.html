About Us

Welcome to Deli Belly, your gateway to the vibrant and rich flavors of North Indian cuisine. Nestled in Pune, Deli Belly is a culinary journey that invites you to experience the true essence of Indian gastronomy.

At Deli Belly, we are passionate about food and believe in the magic of authentic Indian flavors. Our culinary journey began with a vision to bring the warmth and authenticity of North Indian cuisine to our community. With a deep-rooted love for traditional recipes, we embarked on a quest to create a culinary haven where every dish tells a story of heritage and taste.
